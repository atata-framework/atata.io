This is a minor update of Atata with an upgrade of Selenium.WebDriver package reference from v4.39.0 to v4.40.0.
Take a look at [Selenium.WebDriver CHANGELOG](https://github.com/SeleniumHQ/selenium/blob/trunk/dotnet/CHANGELOG)
to find out what was changed there. Among other things, there were important bug fixes there.
